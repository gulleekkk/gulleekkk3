Aktau

Aktau is a western city located in Kazakhstan. In Aktau, located the Caspian Sea. Its name means "white mountain" in Kazakh language. The city was known as Shevchenko from 1964 to 1991. In Aktau, we have beautiful buildings, streets, canyons, and blue sea. Aktau is known for its unique block address system. Almost no streets in Aktau have names; instead, addresses generally consist of three numbers: the district number (also known as micro-region/micro-district/block), the building number, and the apartment number.
